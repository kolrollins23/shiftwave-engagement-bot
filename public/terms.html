Shiftwave Internal Notification Program — Effective June 10, 2026

1. Program Description

The Shiftwave Internal Notification Program sends SMS messages to enrolled Shiftwave team members to alert them when new content is published on Shiftwave's social media accounts (Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Twitter/X, Facebook). Messages may include a link to the post and suggested engagement comments.

2. Eligibility

Participation is limited to current Shiftwave employees and contractors who have been added to the notification list by an authorized program administrator.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on Shiftwave's posting schedule. Participants can expect approximately 1–5 messages per week. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

4. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of the notification program at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further notifications will be sent. To re-enroll, contact your program administrator.

5. Help & Support

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact your Shiftwave program administrator directly. You can also reach us via the notification management app at social-engagement-bot.vercel.app.

6. Participant Responsibilities

Participants are responsible for notifying the program administrator if their phone number changes. Shiftwave is not responsible for messages delivered to an outdated number.

7. Program Changes

Shiftwave reserves the right to modify or discontinue this notification program at any time. Participants will be notified of material changes via SMS.
