Employee SMS Notification Program

This page provides official, public information about the internal SMS notification program operated by Cofactor Systems, Inc. (doing business as Shiftwave). It documents the sender, the messaging use case, the opt-in process, and the required consumer disclosures for our A2P 10DLC messaging registration.

Who operates this program

Cofactor Systems, Inc. is the company that operates the Shiftwave brand. Our consumer product and company information is published at shiftwave.co. This SMS program is a separate, internal employee communication tool run by the same company.

What this program does

When Shiftwave publishes new content on its official social media accounts (LinkedIn, Instagram, X, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube), enrolled employees receive an SMS from (805) 456-3308 letting them know and inviting them to engage with the post. This is a closed, internal program — messages are sent only to current Shiftwave employees and contractors who have enrolled. No messages are sent to the general public.

Program disclosures
  • Sender: Cofactor Systems, Inc. d/b/a Shiftwave, from (805) 456-3308
  • Message frequency: approximately 1–5 messages per week, based on posting activity
  • Cost: message and data rates may apply
  • Opt out: reply STOP to any message at any time
  • Help: reply HELP to any message, or contact your program administrator

How employees opt in

Enrollment is voluntary and is not a condition of employment. During onboarding, an authorized administrator describes the program and its disclosures, and the employee opts in by personally providing their own mobile number and giving written consent, which is recorded with the date and time. No message is sent to anyone who has not personally provided their number and consented. The complete opt-in steps and the exact consent language are documented on the Opt-In Details page below.
