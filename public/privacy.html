Privacy Policy

Cofactor Systems (doing business as “Shiftwave”) — Effective June 2026

This SMS program and this Privacy Policy are provided by Cofactor Systems, a company that does business as (d/b/a) “Shiftwave.” “Cofactor Systems” and “Shiftwave” are the same company and the same sender of the SMS messages described here. Company website: shiftwave.co. Program information: social-engagement-bot.vercel.app/optin.html.

1. Program Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Cofactor Systems (d/b/a Shiftwave) (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information collected in connection with our internal SMS notification program. This program sends text messages to enrolled Shiftwave employees and contractors from the phone number (805) 456-3308 to notify them when new content is published on Shiftwave’s social media channels. Full opt-in details are available at social-engagement-bot.vercel.app/optin.html.

2. SMS Consent and Mobile Information — No Sharing for Marketing

Cofactor Systems (d/b/a Shiftwave) does not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in information, or messaging consent to any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

SMS consent obtained for this program is used solely to send the internal employee notifications described in this policy. Participation is voluntary, and you may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Message frequency is approximately 1–5 messages per week; message and data rates may apply.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from employees and contractors enrolled in the notification program:

  • Name
  • Mobile phone number

This information is provided directly by the employee at enrollment and is stored securely.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your name and phone number are used exclusively to send you SMS notifications about Shiftwave’s social media posts. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond the internal team communications described here.

5. Information Sharing

As stated in Section 2, we do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Your phone number is shared only with Twilio, Inc., our SMS delivery provider, solely to deliver messages to you. Twilio’s privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

6. Data Retention

Your contact information is retained for as long as you are an active participant in the notification program. You may request removal at any time by replying STOP to any message or contacting your program administrator.

7. Security

Contact information is stored using encrypted environment variables on Vercel’s infrastructure. Access to the notification system is password-protected and limited to authorized Cofactor Systems (Shiftwave) staff.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy or to request removal from the program, contact your Shiftwave program administrator or reply STOP to any notification message. This program is operated by Cofactor Systems (d/b/a Shiftwave). See also our Terms of Service and Opt-In Details.
