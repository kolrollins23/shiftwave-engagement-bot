Privacy Policy

Cofactor Systems, Inc. (operating as Shiftwave) — Effective June 2026

1. Program Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Cofactor Systems, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"), operating as Shiftwave, handles information collected in connection with our internal SMS notification program. This program sends text messages to enrolled Shiftwave employees from the phone number (805) 456-3308 to notify them when new content is published on Shiftwave's social media channels. Full opt-in details are available at social-engagement-bot.vercel.app/optin.html.

1a. SMS Consent

We will not share your mobile phone number or SMS consent with any third party for marketing purposes. SMS consent obtained for this program is used solely to send internal employee notifications as described in this policy. Participation is voluntary and employees may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from team members who are enrolled in the notification program:

  • Name
  • Mobile phone number

This information is provided directly by team members or their manager and is stored securely.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your name and phone number are used exclusively to send you SMS notifications about Shiftwave's social media posts. We do not use your information for marketing to external parties, advertising, or any purpose beyond internal team communications.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your phone number is shared only with Twilio, Inc., our SMS delivery provider, solely for the purpose of delivering messages to you. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

5. Data Retention

Your contact information is retained for as long as you are an active participant in the notification program. You may request removal at any time by replying STOP to any message or contacting your program administrator.

6. Security

Contact information is stored using encrypted environment variables on Vercel's infrastructure. Access to the notification system is password-protected and limited to authorized Shiftwave staff.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy or to request removal from the program, contact your Shiftwave program administrator or reply STOP to any notification message.
