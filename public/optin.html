Program Description

Cofactor Systems, Inc. (d/b/a Shiftwave) operates an internal SMS notification program to alert employees and contractors when new content is published on Shiftwave’s official social media accounts (LinkedIn, Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube). Messages are sent from the phone number (805) 456-3308.

This is a closed, internal program. Messages are sent only to current Cofactor Systems, Inc. (Shiftwave) employees and contractors. No messages are sent to members of the public.

How Employees Opt In

Enrollment in this program is entirely voluntary, is not a condition of employment, and is admin-managed. There is one opt-in path, and it works as follows:

  1. During onboarding, an authorized Cofactor Systems, Inc. (Shiftwave) administrator describes the SMS notification program to the employee, including its purpose, the message frequency (approximately 1–5 messages per week), that message and data rates may apply, and that the employee may reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help.
  2. The employee voluntarily provides their own personal mobile phone number and agrees to the consent statement below, giving explicit consent to receive SMS notifications.
  3. The administrator records the employee’s name, mobile number, the consent language shown to them, and the date and time consent was given in the internal notification system.
  4. No messages are sent to any employee until they have personally provided their phone number and agreed to the consent statement below.
Consent language provided to employees at enrollment:

“By providing your mobile phone number, you agree to receive SMS notifications from Cofactor Systems, Inc. (d/b/a Shiftwave) when new content is posted on Shiftwave’s social media channels. Message frequency varies based on posting activity (typically 1–5 messages per week). Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.”

Message Content

Messages sent through this program include:

  • A notification that a new post has been published on a specific platform
  • A direct link to the social media post
  • Optional AI-generated comment suggestions to help employees engage
  • Instructions for logging engagement points via SMS reply (LIKE, COMMENT, or BOTH)

Example message:
“Shiftwave: New LinkedIn post! Engage here: https://linkedin.com/company/shiftwave. Reply LIKE (1pt), COMMENT (2pt), or BOTH (3pt). Msg&Data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.”

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on Shiftwave’s social media posting schedule. Employees can expect approximately 1–5 messages per week.

How to Opt Out

  • Reply STOP to any message — no further messages will be sent
  • Or contact your Cofactor Systems, Inc. (Shiftwave) administrator to be removed from the list

After opting out via STOP, a single confirmation message will be sent and no further notifications will follow. Reply START to re-subscribe.

Help & Support

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact your Cofactor Systems, Inc. (Shiftwave) program administrator directly.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.
