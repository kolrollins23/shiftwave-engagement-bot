Employee SMS Notification Program

Cofactor Systems, Inc. (operating as Shiftwave) — Effective June 2026

Program Description

Cofactor Systems / Shiftwave operates an internal SMS notification program to alert employees when new content is published on Shiftwave's official social media accounts (LinkedIn, Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube). Messages are sent from the phone number (805) 456-3308.

This is a closed, internal program. Messages are sent only to current Shiftwave employees and contractors. No messages are sent to members of the public.

How Employees Opt In

Enrollment in this program is entirely voluntary and admin-managed. The opt-in process is as follows:

  1. An authorized Shiftwave administrator verbally or in writing informs the employee about the SMS notification program and its purpose.
  2. The employee voluntarily provides their personal mobile phone number to the administrator, indicating consent to receive SMS notifications.
  3. The administrator manually enters the employee's name and phone number into the internal notification system.
  4. No messages are sent to any employee until they have explicitly provided their phone number and consented to enrollment.
Consent language provided to employees at enrollment:

"By providing your mobile phone number, you agree to receive SMS notifications from Shiftwave (Cofactor Systems) when new content is posted on Shiftwave's social media channels. Message frequency varies based on posting activity (typically 1–5 messages per week). Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance."

Message Content

Messages sent through this program include:

  • A notification that a new post has been published on a specific platform
  • A direct link to the social media post
  • Optional AI-generated comment suggestions to help employees engage
  • Instructions for logging engagement points via SMS reply (LIKE, COMMENT, or BOTH)

Example message:
"Shiftwave just posted on LinkedIn! Check it out and engage: https://linkedin.com/company/shiftwave. Reply LIKE (1pt), COMMENT (2pt), or BOTH (3pt) to log your points. Reply STOP to opt out."

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on Shiftwave's social media posting schedule. Employees can expect approximately 1–5 messages per week.

How to Opt Out

Employees may opt out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message — no further messages will be sent
  • Contacting their Shiftwave administrator to be removed from the list

After opting out via STOP, a single confirmation message will be sent and no further notifications will follow.

Help & Support

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact your Shiftwave program administrator directly.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Privacy

Employee phone numbers are stored securely and used solely for this notification program. Data is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes. See our full Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
